Welcome to Coins Game

  • There will be a robot by which you will be collecting coins.
  • There will be both positive and negative coins.
  • Positive and negative coins are of yellow and black color respectively.
  • Postive coins = +1, Negative coins = -1.
  • There will be a time limit of 2 minutes.
  • Both the time limit and score will be displayed on the screen.
  • Right SelectStart of controller - Forward
  • Left SelectStart of controller - Backward
  • Right SqueezeStart of controller - Right
  • Left SqueezeStart of controller - Left
